JOB TITLE: DevOps / Platform Engineer
LOCATION: Remote (India only)
EMPLOYMENT TYPE: Full-time

ABOUT THE COMPANY:
We are a cloud-native startup building a developer productivity platform. Our customers are engineering teams at mid-to-large enterprises. We are looking for a DevOps engineer to build and maintain our AWS infrastructure.

RESPONSIBILITIES:
- Design, implement, and manage AWS infrastructure using Terraform (Infrastructure as Code)
- Build and maintain Kubernetes clusters (EKS) for microservices deployment
- Set up CI/CD pipelines using GitHub Actions, ArgoCD, or Jenkins
- Implement observability stack (Prometheus, Grafana, Loki, Tempo)
- Manage secrets and access control using Vault and IAM policies
- Optimize cloud costs and resource utilization
- Participate in incident response and blameless post-mortems

REQUIRED SKILLS (Must-have):
- 4+ years of DevOps or SRE experience
- Strong experience with AWS (EC2, EKS, RDS, S3, VPC, IAM)
- Proficiency with Terraform or Pulumi
- Hands-on experience with Kubernetes (EKS, kops, or vanilla K8s)
- Experience with CI/CD tools (GitHub Actions, GitLab CI, or Jenkins)
- Knowledge of containerization (Docker, container security)
- Scripting skills in Bash or Python

NICE-TO-HAVE SKILLS:
- Kubernetes certifications (CKA, CKAD, CKS)
- Experience with service mesh (Istio, Linkerd)
- Knowledge of GitOps (ArgoCD, Flux)
- Experience with monitoring tools (Datadog, New Relic)
- Experience with security scanning (Trivy, Falco, OPA)

EDUCATION & EXPERIENCE:
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field (or equivalent experience)
- 4-8 years of DevOps/SRE experience

WHAT WE OFFER:
- Competitive salary (₹22-38 LPA)
- 100% remote with home office setup allowance
- Annual cloud certification reimbursement (AWS, CKA, etc.)
- 30 days of paid time off
