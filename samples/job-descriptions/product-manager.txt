JOB TITLE: Product Manager - B2B SaaS
LOCATION: Gurgaon, India (On-site)
EMPLOYMENT TYPE: Full-time

ABOUT THE COMPANY:
We are a B2B SaaS company building an AI-powered customer support platform. Our product is used by 200+ mid-market companies to reduce ticket volume and improve CSAT scores.

RESPONSIBILITIES:
- Define product vision, strategy, and roadmap for one of our core product lines
- Conduct customer interviews, surveys, and usability testing to identify pain points
- Write detailed PRDs (Product Requirements Documents) with user stories and acceptance criteria
- Prioritize backlog using frameworks like RICE, MoSCoW, or Kano
- Collaborate with engineering, design, and marketing teams to ship features
- Define and track success metrics (KPIs) such as adoption, retention, and NPS
- Conduct competitive analysis and market research

REQUIRED SKILLS (Must-have):
- 3+ years of product management experience (SaaS experience preferred)
- Strong understanding of agile methodologies (Scrum or Kanban)
- Experience writing PRDs and managing backlogs in JIRA or Asana
- Ability to analyze data using SQL, Mixpanel, Amplitude, or Google Analytics
- Basic understanding of web technologies (REST APIs, frontend/backend concepts)
- Excellent communication and stakeholder management skills

NICE-TO-HAVE SKILLS:
- Experience with AI/ML products or chatbots
- Technical background (CS degree or prior engineering experience)
- Experience with Figma or prototyping tools
- MBA or product management certification (CSPO, Pragmatic Institute)
- Experience with B2B enterprise sales cycles

EDUCATION & EXPERIENCE:
- Bachelor's degree in Business, Computer Science, or related field
- 3-7 years of product management experience
- MBA is a plus but not required

WHAT WE OFFER:
- Competitive salary (₹18-28 LPA)
- Performance-based bonuses (up to 20%)
- Health insurance for family
- Product conference budget (ProductCon, Mind the Product)
