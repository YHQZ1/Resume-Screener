JOB TITLE: Data Scientist - Machine Learning
LOCATION: Pune, India (Hybrid)
EMPLOYMENT TYPE: Full-time

ABOUT THE COMPANY:
We are an e-commerce analytics platform helping D2C brands optimize pricing, inventory, and customer retention. We process over 1 billion events per month and use ML to drive business decisions.

RESPONSIBILITIES:
- Develop and deploy machine learning models for churn prediction, product recommendation, and demand forecasting
- Perform exploratory data analysis (EDA) on large-scale datasets (millions of rows)
- Build feature engineering pipelines and maintain feature stores
- Design and analyze A/B tests to measure model impact
- Collaborate with data engineers to productionize models using MLflow and Kubernetes
- Communicate findings to non-technical stakeholders via dashboards and presentations

REQUIRED SKILLS (Must-have):
- 3+ years of experience in data science or ML engineering
- Strong Python skills (pandas, numpy, scikit-learn, xgboost/lightgbm)
- Experience with SQL for data extraction and aggregation
- Understanding of machine learning algorithms (regression, classification, clustering, time series)
- Familiarity with A/B testing and statistical inference (p-values, confidence intervals)
- Experience with at least one visualization tool (Tableau, Power BI, or matplotlib/seaborn)

NICE-TO-HAVE SKILLS:
- Experience with deep learning (TensorFlow, PyTorch)
- Knowledge of MLOps tools (MLflow, Kubeflow, or DVC)
- Experience with big data technologies (PySpark, Dask, or Snowflake)
- Experience with LLMs or generative AI (LangChain, Hugging Face)
- Kaggle competition experience or published research

EDUCATION & EXPERIENCE:
- Master's or PhD in Data Science, Statistics, Computer Science, or related field
- 3-7 years of industry experience

WHAT WE OFFER:
- Competitive salary (₹18-30 LPA)
- Flexible work hours
- Access to Kaggle competitions (paid entries)
- Conference budget (NeurIPS, ICML, KDD)
